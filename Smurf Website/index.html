Save the Smurfs

It's 2025 and Smurfs remain one of the most endangered species.

Life Saving Advice

WATER CAMOUFLAGE

If being hunted, jump into the nearest body of water. Your blue skin blends perfectly with water, hiding you from predators.

BLUEBERRY DANGER

If you are at a farm and surrounded by animals eating the fruits, stay away from the blueberries. You might be mistaken for a berry!

NIGHTTIME SAFETY

Travel only at night when your blue colour is less visible. Avoid leaving your mushroom during the day unless absolutely necessary.

SAFETY IN MUSHROOMS

Always know the location of the nearest mushroom house. It's the safest place to hide when danger approaches.

STAY IN GROUPS

Smurfs are stronger together. Travel with fellow Smurfs and watch eachother's backs. Two or more is always safer than one.
